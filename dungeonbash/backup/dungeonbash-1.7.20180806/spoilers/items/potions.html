MARTIN'S OFFICIAL DUNGEON BASH POTION SPOILER

For Version 1.7 of Martin's Dungeon Bash

This spoiler describes the behaviour of the potions found in Martin's Dungeon Bash version 1.7.

healing potion

Heals 3-36 points of damage; if the healing exceeds the player's missing hit points, the player gains one maximum hit point.

body potion

Increases the player's Body by one point.

agility potion

Increases the player's Agility by one point.

weakness potion

Drains 1-4 points each of Body and Agility from the player.

poison potion

Does 3-36 points of damage to the player.

restoration potion

Cures all damage to one of Body and Agility (if both are damaged, picks randomly).
